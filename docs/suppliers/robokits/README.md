## 2. Warranty — ⚠️ CRITICAL CONTRADICTION

This is the most important thing to understand before ordering.

**Product page says:** `Warranty : None` — in bold, directly on the product listing.

**Warranty & Return Policy page says:**  
> *"All products sold by Robokits by default are covered under 5 days warranty even if you see warranty none on the product page."*

**Assessment:** These two statements directly contradict each other. In a dispute, Robokits could point to the product page and deny coverage. **Do not rely on the policy page overriding the product page.** If you order, consider this a no-warranty purchase and test everything aggressively on day one.

### What the Warranty Policy Actually Says

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Duration** | 5 days from delivery |
| **What's covered** | Manufacturing defects only — "if any product is found to be not working or not matching the description" |
| **What voids it** | Reverse polarity, overloading, short circuit, ESD, improper handling, any modification |
| **How to claim** | Email `support@robokits.co.in` within 5 days of delivery with problem details + images/video |
| **Resolution** | Exchange (not refund) after RMA approval, within 3-5 working days |
| **RMA required?** | Yes — they issue a Return Merchandise Authorization number |
| **Out-of-warranty repair?** | Yes, at "minimal possible rates" — email them |

**Step 1 — Unbox on video.** Continuous, uncut recording showing the sealed package opening and each component being inspected. This is your evidence if something is wrong.

**Step 2 — Test within 5 days.** Connect the Pixhawk to your computer via USB. Run through:
- ArduPilot/PX4 firmware flash and verification
- All sensor calibrations (accelerometer, gyro, magnetometer, barometer)
- GPS satellite acquisition (NEO-M8N)
- All PWM/servo output channels
- SD card slot and logging

**Step 3 — If defective, email immediately** to `support@robokits.co.in` with:
- Order number (RKI-7234)
- Clear photos of the defect
- Video demonstrating the problem
- Description of what you tested and the results

**Step 4 — Wait for RMA.** Their team reviews and issues an RMA number. They may also decide to replace without requiring return, or refund instead — at their discretion.

## 8. Arthur's Risk Assessment

**Risk Level: MEDIUM-HIGH**

| Risk | Detail | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Warranty contradiction | Product says "None", policy says "5 days" | Film unboxing, test everything on day 1 |
| Counterfeit Pixhawk 2.4.8 | This model is widely cloned with poor IMUs | Verify with Mission Planner / QGC sensor health |
| 15-day ship time | Long delay from order to receipt | Order early; don't wait until you need it |
| 5-day test window | Tight for thorough flight controller validation | Have test setup ready before delivery |
| Return shipping cost | You pay to courier to Gandhinagar | Factor ₹200-500 into risk budget |
| No refund — exchange only | Even if approved, you get replacement not money back | Accept that this is not Amazon |

**Bottom line:** This is a functional purchase, not a premium experience. The Pixhawk 2.4.8 will likely work, but you have no real warranty safety net. Test aggressively and immediately on arrival.
